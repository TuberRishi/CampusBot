College Name: Example University
Circular Ref: EU/FIN/2025/07-01

Date: July 15, 2025

Subject: Deadline for Semester Fee Payment

All students are hereby informed that the deadline for the payment of semester fees for the upcoming Autumn 2025 session is August 31, 2025.

Students can pay the fees through the online portal on the college website.

A late fee of Rs. 500 will be imposed for payments made between September 1 and September 10, 2025. After September 10, a fine of Rs. 100 per day will be applicable.

No fee payments will be accepted after September 20, 2025, and students who have not paid will have their registration cancelled.

For any queries regarding fee payment, please contact the Finance Office.

Registrar
